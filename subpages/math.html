$$\left(\sum_{i = 1}^n a_i^2 \right) \left(\sum_{i = 1}^n b_i^2 \right) \geq \left(\sum_{i = 1}^n a_i b_i \right)^2$$

QM-AM-GM-HM Inequality

Statement

Consider a sequence of positive reals

\( (x_i)_{i \in I} \)

with

\( I \subset \mathbb{N} \)

. It holds that:

$$\sqrt{\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i = 1}^n x_i^2} \geq \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i = 1}^n x_i \geq \sqrt[n]{\prod_{i = 1}^n x_i} \geq \frac{n}{\sum_{i = 1}^n \frac{1}{x_i}}$$
